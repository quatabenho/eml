    About Us

    Learn More About Us

    For every lunch bought by anyone
    A lunch is given to a kid at school.

    Ensure that no child at school goes hungry, starting with kids right here in our own backyard

    • Since June 2015, EML has provided over 475,000 lunches to hungry Kiwi kids.
    • Delivering 1,400 lunches daily to kids attending 48 schools across Auckland, Hamilton and Wellington.
    • Our orders have grown from 500 lunches per day to more than 2,000.

    Our mission is simple yet profound: to ensure no child at school goes hungry. With approximately 1 in 4 Kiwi kids living in poverty, we believe in making a tangible difference.

    Why Choose EML?

    Every meal you buy is matched with a meal for a child in need.

    When you choose Eat My Lunch, you're not only indulging in delicious, freshly prepared meals, but you're also contributing to a powerful social cause.
